Used PC parts FAQ

Where can I buy used PC parts in Europe?

Search established regional marketplaces and compare listings through PCPartDeals. Useful sources include Vinted, Kleinanzeigen, Leboncoin, Wallapop, Yaga and Osta, depending on your country.

Is PCPartDeals a marketplace or an aggregator?

PCPartDeals primarily helps users discover and compare external marketplace listings. Users may submit relevant listings, but purchases generally take place on the original marketplace.

Is it safe to buy a second-hand graphics card?

It can be safe when you verify the seller, request testing evidence, inspect the card description and use the marketplace's protected payment process. Avoid offers that are implausibly cheap.

Can I find used CPUs, RAM and storage?

Yes. PCPartDeals covers CPUs, GPUs, motherboards, RAM, HDDs and SSDs, with filters that help narrow the live listing feed by category and marketplace.
