How to avoid used GPU scams

How to avoid getting scammed when buying GPUs and stay away from losing money.

Published: May 2026 5 min read

Biggest red flags to look out for

When buying used PC hardware, you must look out for anything suspicious in the marketplace listings. The biggest red flags have to be: way lower price compared to other listings, short descriptions of the product and seller's account age.

To avoid this, you must always do some background checks like: reading seller's reviews, looking at their other listings and checking account age. Account age shouldn't be under 1-2 months and if it is then it might suggest that the user could be a robot or scammer.

We reccomend asking the user about the performance and benchmarks of the hardware they are selling. Screenshots and images are really useful for that purpose.
